About Us

Chongwe Mixed Farm is located in the heart of Chongwe District, Zambia. Our farm is committed to sustainable agriculture, combining crop farming, livestock rearing, and agroforestry to create a holistic farming ecosystem. We aim to contribute to food security, provide fresh produce, and promote environmentally friendly practices.

Our Activities

  • Crops: We grow maize, soybeans, vegetables, and more.
  • Livestock: We rear pigs, chickens, goats, and cattle.
  • Agroforestry: Planting and maintaining fruit trees and sustainable woodlots.
